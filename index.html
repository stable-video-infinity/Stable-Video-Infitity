Stable Video Infinity

All the following videos are generated end-to-end without post-processing, including smooth scene transitions. (Videos have been heavily compressed)

Stable Video Infinity (SVI) is able to generate infinite-length videos featuring temporally consistent quality and visually plausible scene transitions, which supports streaming text control (storyline) and multimodal conditions, e.g., audio and skeleton, making long-form video production, robotic simulation, and world generation easy.

Consistent Video Generation

Use one text prompt to control the motion and scene dynamics of the entire video sequence

This setting aims to generate temporally coherent videos in a homogeneous scene controlled by a single text prompt, which aligns with the previous long video definition. Our SVI-Shot demonstrates superior temporal consistency and visual quality compared to existing works, maintaining coherent motion and scene dynamics throughout the entire video sequence while preserving fine-grained details and realistic object-environment interactions.

Creative Video Generation

Use a text prompt stream to generate short films with specific storylines, where each 5-second clip is controlled by one unique text prompt

This setting targets the needs of vloggers (e.g., TikTok) by emphasizing storytelling with plausible scene transitions. Our SVI-Film excels in generating videos with diverse scenes and coherent storylines, outperforming existing methods in both visual quality and temporal consistency. It effectively captures the essence of each prompt while ensuring smooth transitions between different scenes, resulting in engaging and visually appealing short films that maintain narrative flow and cinematic quality throughout the sequence.
